To whoever is running a team right now,

The team you build is the company you build. Especially early on, when you have very few dollars and you're competing in the world's most competitive market — the market for people.

After I sold my last company, I knew exactly what I wanted to fix next. People are the biggest cost on any balance sheet — and everyone is trying to cut bloat without knowing where it actually lives.

The problem I kept running into: I never really knew who was good. I went on vibes. We overpaid people who should have been fired and underpaid the ones carrying the company. Nobody could prove it either way.

Is my highest-paid engineer actually my best one?

Did the person who quit last month carry three others without me knowing?

Unmask gives everyone an honest grade based on what they actually do. No surveys. No gut feel. Just the truth.
